Privacy Policy

Thank you for using Gooin! We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information while using our app. Please read the following to understand how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Information We Collect

When using Gooin, we may collect the following information:

  • Email Address: For account registration, login, and service notifications.
  • Nickname: For personalized display and social interactions.
  • Location Information: We do not track your real-time location. We only record the event address you provide when participating in or posting an event to show nearby activities.

Purpose of Using the Information

  • To provide service functions such as event publishing, participation, and search.
  • To display nearby events based on the provided event address.
  • To send essential service notifications (e.g., event reminders, system messages).

We do not use your personal information for advertising, sale, transfer, or any non-service-related purposes.

Data Control and Deletion

  • You can view, edit, or delete your personal information in your account settings at any time.
  • To completely delete your account and all data, use the in-app settings or contact our customer support email.
  • Once deleted, the data cannot be recovered.
